Info taken from the patch 1.17.2 forum post.

Pet Ability Value Changes

  • Furious Howl — Focus cost reduced from 60 to 50, and range increased from 15 to 40.
  • Thunderstomp — Cooldown reduced from 1 minute to 40 seconds. 1.5× threat modifier added.
  • Scorpid Poison — Focus cost reduced from 30 to 25. Cooldown reduced from 4s to 3s. Base damage increased by 20%.
  • Shell Shield — Attack speed reduction reduced from 43% to 35%.
  • Bite — Cooldown reduced from 10 seconds to 6 seconds.
  • Screech — Now has a 5 second cooldown.
  • Growl — Now costs 10 Focus, down from 15.
  • Dash — Added to Raptors.

Pet Ability Rank Additions

  • Charge (Boar) — Rank 4 added.
  • Lightning Breath — Rank 1 added.
  • Thunderstomp — Rank 4 added.

Pet Scaling Values (mostly relevant for BM hunters via Spirit Bond Talent)

Spell Power Scaling

  • Poison Spit (Serpent) — 0.25 per tick.
  • Lightning Breath (Wind Serpent) — 0.5.
  • Scorpid Poison (Scorpid) — 0.1 per tick (totalling 0.5 at 5 stacks).
  • Thunderstomp (Gorilla) — 0.21.

Attack Power Scaling

  • Bite — 1 damage per 20 Attack Power.
  • Claw — 1 damage per 42 Attack Power.
  • Screech — 1 damage per 25 Attack Power.
  • Furious Howl — 1 damage per 25 Attack Power.
  • Charge (Boar) — Grants additional Attack Power equal to 35% of the boar's Attack Power.

Other

  • Pets now benefit from Windfury Totem.

New Base Pet Family Abilities

  • Bear — Reduces party damage taken by 8% and increases damage dealt by 3%. 50 focus. 12 second duration. 2 minute cooldown.
  • Crab — Absorbs 335 damage; on breaking or expiration, deals 72–84 damage to nearby enemies. 20 focus. 8 second duration. 30 second cooldown.
  • Crocolisk — Slows target by 80% and deals 60 damage every 0.5s over 2.5s. Deals 25% more damage below 35% health. 50 focus. 30 second cooldown.
  • Hyena — Summons another hyena to assist for 20 seconds, mirroring all pet commands. 50 focus. 1.2 minute cooldown.
  • Raptor — Deals 27–36 damage and applies a bleed for 64 damage over 4 seconds (crits extend bleed by 1 second). 35 focus. 10 second cooldown.
  • Spider — 2 second root, followed by 60% slow for 2 seconds. 50 focus. 1 minute cooldown.
  • Tallstrider — 20% HP and 10% mana regen aura for the party.
